Cardiological Hospital in Bryukhovichi
About project
The project of a cardiological hospital near Lviv involves the construction of a highly specialized cardiological hospital. It consists of an operating unit, an intensive care unit, and a center for restorative medicine.The site allocated for the construction of the hospital is in a pine forest, and we envisage construction in two stages. The first is the construction of a medical center, and the second is a sanatorium.
